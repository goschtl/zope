Developer Resources
-------------------

- Subversion browser:

  http://svn.zope.org/zope.app/

- Read-only Subversion checkout:

  $ svn co svn://svn.zope.org/repos/main/zope.app/trunk

- Writable Subversion checkout:

  $ svn co svn+ssh://userid@svn.zope.org/repos/main/zope.app/trunk
