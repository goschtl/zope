Zope Foundation Solution Provider Members
=========================================

Zope Solution Provider Members are organizations which offer products and
services built with Zope.

Solutions Provider Members are represented on the board by Solution
Provider Directors. Solution Provider can elect at least one Solution
Provider Director on the board for every three Strategic Developer
Directors, and always at least one Solution Provider
Director. Election occurs annually using the Single Transferable Vote
process.
