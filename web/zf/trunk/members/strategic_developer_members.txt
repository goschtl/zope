Zope Foundation Strategic Developer Members
===========================================

A Strategic Developer Member is an organization that uses Zope to
develop its own products or services, and for which the Zope platform
is of strategic interest. Strategic Developer Members maintain two or
more `Committer Members`_ on their staff.

Each Strategic Developer member is entitled to appoint one
representative to the Zope Foundation Board (a Strategic Developer
Director).
